Timer2 – Keyboard and push buttons (backlight?)
Timer0 – Pipeline LCD
Z(30:31) – Reserved for turntable
Sreg (000 D MM PP) – r29 : D = Direction of rotation (0 clockwise 1 counterclockwise) P = Power (1-3)  M = Menu 
Menu:
* 00 running
* 01 Entry
* 10 Paused
* 11 Finished
Minutes/seconds – r27:r28
LCD time – r26
< r26 – anything!
